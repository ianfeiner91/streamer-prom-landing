The Venue

Live From
Andretti Katy

New for 2026: prom night gets a racetrack. The whole building becomes the set.

Andretti Indoor Karting & Games — Katy, TX

Streamer Prom 2026 takes over an ~88,000-square-foot indoor playground on Houston's west side — built around a half-mile, multi-level electric superkart track with banked turns, long straightaways, and sweeping downward spirals. Creators in prom fits, doing 35 mph, on camera. That's the energy.

Between crownings there are 80+ arcade games, a two-story laser tag arena, three VR experiences, an indoor ropes course with a zip line, and mini golf — every corner of it streamable, clippable content. A full restaurant, two bars, and 4,600+ square feet of private event space seal the takeover.
